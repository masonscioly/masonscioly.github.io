MASON INVITE



Welcome to the 2023 Mason Invitational, and the first ever in-person Mason Invitational! See below for block schedules, logistics, registration, and payment information regarding this year's Mason Invite.

REGISTRATION + SCHEDULE

The 2023 Mason Invite will take place Saturday, November 11th. All official events will be offered (plus two refreshing trial events!), and science-themed prizes will be raffled off to one of the top 6 teams in each event.

If your school is interested in attending, please fill out this registration form. Thank you, and we hope to host your school this year!

SCHOOL MAP + PARKING

Buses can drop off at the main doors in the front but should park in the bus circle at the back of the building past the community center and athletic wing. Cars can park by the athletic center and enter through the music door, then walk straight down the hallway until reaching the large commons.

Please keep in mind that we will only be using A, C, and D Pods of the school for this invite and since we have 12 identical pods, it is important to ensure that you always know where you are in relation to the main help areas. Thanks!
