Update 2: August 15th, 2025

Registration for the 5th annual Mason Satellite Invitational is open! Registration will be done via Scilympiad, which can be found here. The registration fee is $85 each for the first two teams from a school and $65 for any subsequent teams.

Payment is open as well. Payment information will be emailed directly to registered teams and can also be found on our Scilympiad pages.

All information about the tournament, including event supervisors, event modifications, and tournament updates, will be emailed and posted to our website. We would also like to provide you with our official Tournament Schedule, detailing event modifications, appeals, and the awards ceremony. We will be running all 23 B/C events, along with 5 trial events per division, so stay on the lookout for those to be announced!

You may view previous communications via the coach communications document our website as well. If there are any other emails you would like to add to receive updates from our tournament, please fill out our email list form. As always, feel free to reach out if you have any questions or concerns!

Update 1: July 1st, 2025

We’re excited to announce the 5th Annual Mason Science Olympiad Invitational, running via a Satellite format on Scilympiad from November 15th to the 23rd. These dates give teams 2 full weekends to take the tests, along with the week in between. There is no limit to the number of teams per school or the number of teams that can attend this tournament.

We will be running all 23 events in both Division B and C, along with many fun trial events, so stay on the lookout for those to be announced. Some of the trial events we have run in the past include our famous Trivia (Taylor’s Version), Pokethology, Supercell Showdown, Bot, K-Pop Trivia, and many more!

Additionally, the top 6 teams in each event will be raffled off to win some fun prizes, which will be shipped out along with the awards (see our list for 2022 here).

For a general indication of test quality, feel free to check out our tournament archive. Tests are primarily written by coaches, alumni, Mason Scioly, and other volunteers. Like in previous years, all tests, keys, and histograms will be publicly released after the competition period has concluded.

The cost of registration for this tournament is $85 each for the first two teams, and $65 for any subsequent teams, with no limit to the number of teams from one school that can register. The registration will be done through Scilympiad and will open up later this fall.

Finally, we are looking for some Alumni volunteers or High School writers with a history of writing quality tests to help us run the tournament, the application for which can be found here. High Schoolers whose school is competing for Division C will be allowed to write tests for Division B, so feel free to apply.

For any questions, please contact masonscio@gmail.com and seilers@masonohioschools.com. Future announcements will be posted to our coach communications document.

Thank you for your time! We hope to see you at Mason Invitational this year!
