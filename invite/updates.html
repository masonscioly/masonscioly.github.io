UPDATE 5 (October 19th)

We are officially 4 weeks away from the start of the 2025 Mason Satellite Invitational; at this moment approximately 180 teams have registered (you can find a full list here). Here are a couple of logistical updates.

We realize that many coaches/students competing may not have used the Scilympiad platform for a couple of seasons. If you need a refresher on how to navigate the platform, the head coach guide can be found here, and the student guide can be found here. If you have any issues with Scilympiad, be sure to reach out.

Awards Ceremony: The awards ceremony for our tournament will be on Sunday, December 1st, with the pre-awards ceremony beginning at 6:30 PM, the Division B awards beginning at 7:00 PM, and the Division C awards beginning at 8:00 PM. All times listed are in EST. We will be recognizing the top 6 teams in each event, as well as the top 6 schools overall in each division. If multiple teams from the same school place within the top 6 overall, we will only consider the higher placing team. Additionally, the top 6 teams from each event will be entered into a special raffle to win one of our special event prizes which will be revealed during the awards ceremony. Our list of previous awards can be found here.

Our official results will be published on Duosmium following the ceremony.

Team Names/Numbers: For every team that is competing at our invitational, a team name must be selected. You can do this by going into the Head Coach Dasboard, pressing the “Register/Update Teams” button, and then pressing the “Edit” button. When you do this, please do not keep your school name in the team name. For example, if your team names are White and Orange, please leave your team name as “White” instead of “Mason High School White”. You may set your team name to anything, though please be aware that we will have to attempt to say these names during the awards ceremony.

Team numbers will be initially assigned on November 1st. At this time, you will be able to go into Scilympiad and assign students to certain teams/events to prepare for the competition to begin. Event Modifications: The Event Modifications document has had a couple slight changes since our last communication summarized below (as well as some helpful reminders):

  • The Materials list for Write It Do It B has been changed to include more materials other than notebook paper
  • For Optics C, competitors will need a straightedge, as well as a printed copy (in color) of the image sheet provided.
  • For Codebusters B/C, 3 copies of the reference sheet as well as a copy of certain problems from the test must be printed and given to competitors. This document does not need to be in color, and will be published by November 9th.
  • Materials lists for Experimental Design B/C have been posted, please note that not all materials will be allowed for the experiment and a subset of those materials will be provided on the Scilympiad test. Please instruct your students to read this list carefully, as it will be a 0.95x multiplier to their score if they utilize materials that are not contained on this subset.
  • All test events are being run using state/national level topics as provided on the rules. All build events will be using invitational/regional level rules as listed.
Event Supervisors: Our Event Supervisor list can be found here for Division B and here for Division C. Short introductions for each supervisor as well as a picture will be posted on our website shortly.

Payment: Just as a reminder, payment for our Invitational is due by November 15th. If we have not received your payment prior to this date, your teams will not be eligible to compete. Instructions for payment, as well as an Invoice, have been sent out to all registered teams. Once payment is received, your teams’ status will be updated on Scilympiad, as well as a confirmation email. Checks may take a couple days to process through our system, so please send payment as soon as possible to ensure we receive it before the start of the tournament. If you require a W-9 form to process payment, please reply to this email.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to masoninvitational@gmail.com. If you wish to add another email to our email list, please fill out this form. If you wish to be removed from this list, please reply to this email. All updates can be found on our coach communications document, and our tournament schedule can be found here.

UPDATE 1 (June 7th)

We’re excited to announce the 4th Annual Mason Science Olympiad Invitational , running via a Satellite format on Scilympiad from November 16th to the 24th . These dates give teams 2 full weekends to take the tests, along with the week in between. There is no limit to the number of teams per school or number of teams that can attend this tournament.

We will be running all 23 events in both Division B and C , along with many fun trial events so stay on the lookout for those to be announced. Some of the trial events we have run in the past include our famous Trivia (Taylor’s Version), Pokethology, Astrology, and many more!

Additionally, the top 6 teams in each event will be raffled off to win some fun prizes which will be shipped out along with the awards (see our list for 2022 here)

For a general indication of test quality, feel free to check out our tournament archive here. Tests are primarily written by coaches, alumni, Mason Scioly, and other volunteers. Like in previous years, all tests, keys, and histograms will be publicly released after the competition period has concluded.

The cost of registration for this tournament is $80 for the first two teams, and $60 any subsequent teams, with no limit to the amount of teams from one school which can register. The registration form will be done through Scilympiad, and will open up later this fall.

Finally, we are looking for some Alumni volunteers or High School writers with a history of writing quality tests to help us run the tournament, the application for which can be found here High Schoolers whose school is competing for Division C will be allowed to write tests for Division B, so feel free to apply.

For any questions, please contact masonscio@gmail.com and seilers@masonohioschools.com. Future announcements will be posted to our website and this document.

Thank you for your time! We hope to see you at Mason Invitational this year!
