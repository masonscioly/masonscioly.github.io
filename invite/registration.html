Click on the Scilympiad button above to be directed to the registration page for this year's invitational! Registration for this years invitational is $85 each for the first two teams from a school, and $65 each for any subsequent teams from a school. There is currently no limit to the amount of teams from one school or the amount of schools that can participate in our invitational this year. Registration is due on Scilympiad by 11:59 PM on November 9th, with payment due by November 15th. Any questions about registration can be directed to masonscio@gmail.com.
